Maus

By Art Spiegelman

English Work

Create, in groups, a product derived from one of the texts we worked this bimester (Monica's gang/Maus).
A product is an independent piece of work that speaks for itself.

Examples of products you may create:

- podcast
- infographic
- blog post
- letter
- video discussion

Choose one of them.
Work with critical information you got from the readings and discussions in class.
You don't have to necessarily discuss the comic - you may discuss something related to it.

Due to: May 30
